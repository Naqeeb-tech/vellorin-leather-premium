Our Lineage

VELLORINS was born out of a rebellion against fast-fashion synthetic alternatives. Our lineage is tethered directly to generational tanneries, operating with the belief that leather should acquire a unique, personal patina over time.

Every wallet, bag, and belt is cut from custom full-grain hides, devoid of surface fillers or correction sprays.
