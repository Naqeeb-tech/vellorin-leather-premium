Our Journey & Lineage

VELLORINS was born out of a rebellion against fast fashion synthetic alternatives. Our lineage is tethered directly to generational tanneries, operating with the foundational understanding that leather should not break down—it should acquire a personal storytelling patina.

By shifting standard raw processing pipelines entirely in-house, we secure custom full-grain cuts, completely devoid of correction sprays or surface masking fillers. Every wallet, bag, and shoe cut tells the raw, unfiltered story of the hide it was pulled from.

Rules & Brand Regulations
